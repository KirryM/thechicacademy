Men's Styles

Discussions about body types should be approached with sensitivity and an understanding that individuals vary widely in their unique characteristics.
Human bodies are diverse, and categorizing them into specific types can be overly simplistic.

Male Body Types:

  • Ectomorph: Similar to ectomorphic women, ectomorphic men have a slim and lean physique.
    They often have a faster metabolism, making it challenging for them to gain weight or muscle mass.

  • Mesomorph: Mesomorphic men typically have a more muscular and athletic build.
    They tend to have a square or rectangular body shape, with well-defined muscles and a naturally higher muscle mass.

  • Endomorph: Endomorphic men often have a softer and rounder body type, with a higher percentage of body fat.
    They may have a rounder face and carry excess weight in the midsection.

    • Moreover, individual body types can change over time due to factors such as diet, exercise, and lifestyle.
    Embracing body positivity and appreciating the uniqueness of each person's body is essential for fostering a healthy and inclusive perspective on physical appearance.

    Tips on how to dress your body type: Ectomorph (Slim and Lean):
  • Tops: Fitted shirts and layers add dimension.
  • Bottoms: Slim or straight-leg trousers complement a slim build
  • Suits: Tailored suits provide structure.

    • Mesomorph (Muscular and Athletic):
  • Tops: Fitted shirts highlight muscular physique.
  • Bottoms: Straight or slim-cut pants balance the figure.
  • Suits: Well-tailored suits enhance the broad shoulders.

    • Endomorph (Solid and Fuller Figure):
  • Tops: Layering can create a streamlined look.
  • Bottoms: Straight-leg or relaxed-fit pants offer comfort.
  • Suits: Single-breasted suits with a structured cut flatter.
